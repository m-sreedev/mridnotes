Korvais are pieces played by giving importance to the <a href = "{{site.baseurl}}/docs/theory/phrases/matra/"> Matras </a> in 
<a href = "{{site.baseurl}}/docs/theory/phrases/talam/">Talams</a>. <a href = "{{site.baseurl}}/docs/theory/phrases/korvais/"> Read more here. </a>

<hr>

<p style = "line-height: 1.5;">

Korvais are  sorted by Talams they are generally played in and their Matras for organization purposes
<p> <b> Just click on any Matra to jump to the section.</b></p>
I have also  tried to link the variations of the Korvai, if it has any. If you can create one and want me to add it to the collection, feel free to drop a <a href = "mailto:sreedevmelemadom@gmail.com.">mail.</a> <br><br>
Credits are given to the people from whom I had heard the piece for the first time<br>
