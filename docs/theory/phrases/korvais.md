<h2> Korvais </h2>

Korvais are pieces played by giving importance to the <a href = "{{site.baseurl}}/docs/theory/phrases/matra/"> Matras </a> in <a href = "{{site.baseurl}}/docs/theory/phrases/talam/"> Talams </a>.
They are mathematical pieces, usually played 3 times per piece during the Thaniavarthanam. A Thani typically contains more than one Korvai.<br>

A Korvai has two parts - Purvangam, Utharangam.
Purvangam is the part played first and Utharangam is played after.
Utharangam is usually Pancha-Jaathi ThaThiKiTaThom.<br>

Korvais can be played in any <a href = "{{site.baseurl}}/docs/theory/phrases/gathi/"> Gathi </a> - Tisram,Chathurasram,Ghantam, Misram, Sankeernam etc.  <br>
